Pumpbot

node repo | mongodb repo

A Slackbot that posts a random 'Tip of the Day' from a database to the #announcements channel each morning of the workweek. The bot is configured to keep track of what tips have and haven't been posted to ensure there are no repeats posted before the entire list has been used.
